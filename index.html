Critical Security Notice

NovaWallet support will NEVER ask for your 12-word recovery phrase, private keys, or passwords. Anyone asking for this information is a scammer. Keep your seed phrase offline and secure at all times.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common issues.

How do I restore my wallet if I lost my device?
You can restore your wallet on a new device by downloading the NovaWallet app, selecting "I already have a wallet", and entering your 12-word recovery phrase. Ensure you are in a private environment and no one is watching your screen.
Why is my transaction pending for so long?
Transactions depend on network congestion and the gas fee you selected. If the network is busy, transactions with lower fees may take longer to be confirmed by miners/validators. You can check the status using your transaction hash (TXID) on a blockchain explorer.
Can NovaWallet reverse a transaction?
No. Cryptocurrency transactions are irreversible by design. Once a transaction is broadcasted to the blockchain and confirmed, it cannot be canceled or reversed by NovaWallet or any other entity. Always double-check recipient addresses before sending.
